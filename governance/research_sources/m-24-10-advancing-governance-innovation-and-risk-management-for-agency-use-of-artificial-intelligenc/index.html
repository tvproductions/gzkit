OVERVIEW While AI is improving operations and service delivery across the Federal G overnment , agencies must effectively manage its use. As such , this memorandum establishes new agency requirements and guidance for AI governance , innovation , and risk management, including through specific minimum risk management practices for uses of AI that impact the rights and safety of the public . Managing Risks f rom the Use of AI. While agencies will realize significant benefits from AI, they must also manage a range of risks from the use of AI .
